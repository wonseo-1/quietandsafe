Florin Road Partnership

Florin Road Partnership represents the commercial corridor along Florin Road in south Sacramento, bringing together businesses and community stakeholders around economic activity, local services, and the area’s role as a neighborhood shopping destination.

Preservation Sacramento

Preservation Sacramento reflects the city’s historic-preservation community rather than a conventional residential district, connecting residents and advocates with Sacramento’s older buildings, neighborhoods, and architectural heritage.

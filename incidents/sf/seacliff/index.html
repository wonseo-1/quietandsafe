This site checks the source feed daily; records appear here once they are published in that feed. This page combines records retained for the ~150m grid cells assigned to Seacliff, filtered to the last 90 days — the underlying dataset retains only up to 10 recent records per grid cell, so a cell with few incidents may contribute none here even if it has older history. This is not a complete list of every incident reported in the neighborhood, and covers vehicle-related categories only. An "Arrest" tag reflects the police department's recorded case status, not a finding of guilt.

Quiet & Safe

About

Quiet & Safe helps travelers in 26 US cities decide where to park and where to stay, using public police records. Every count on the site names its window (past 12 months) and its source — no scores we can’t explain, no data we invented.

Where the data comes from

Car break-ins, thefts, robberies and assaults come from each city’s official police open-data portal, rebuilt daily. Tent-camp figures are civilian 311 reports (labeled as such — they are not a crime measure). Parking facilities come from SpotHero’s public listings; hotel locations from OpenStreetMap.

How the site makes money

Some outbound links (bag storage, rental cars, hotels, parking bookings) are partner links that may earn us a commission. Partners never affect how blocks are colored or how results are ranked — rankings state their criterion on the page, and the crime data has a single source: the police record.
